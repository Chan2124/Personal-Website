Gun's N' Roses(Hard Rock)

"Sweet Child o' Mine" by Guns N' Roses, released in 1987, is a love song inspired by Axl Rose's then-girlfriend. The iconic guitar riff and Axl's vocals made it a massive hit, showcasing the band's rock prowess.

Goo Goo Dolls(Alternative Rock)

"Iris" is a song by the Goo Goo Dolls, released in 1998. It gained widespread popularity as the soundtrack for the film "City of Angels." The song's lyrics reflect themes of longing and connection, capturing the essence of a deep emotional bond. Its title refers to the Greek goddess Iris, symbolizing communication between gods and humans. "Iris" became a chart-topping hit and remains a timeless ballad, cherished for its poignant lyrics and melodic beauty.

Bruno Mars(Pop)

"Just the Way You Are" is a love song by Bruno Mars released in 2010. The lyrics express unconditional love, emphasizing acceptance of a person's flaws. The song's melody and heartfelt lyrics contributed to its success, earning Bruno Mars a Grammy Award for Best Male Pop Vocal Performance in 2011.

Chris Brown(R&B, Hip-hop)

"New Flame" is a song by Chris Brown featuring Usher and Rick Ross. Released in 2014, it explores the theme of seeking a fresh start in love. The lyrics tell a story of leaving behind old flames and embracing a new, passionate relationship. With its upbeat tempo and catchy chorus, the song became a popular anthem for those looking to ignite a new spark in their romantic lives.

BINI(P-Pop)

""Karera" by BINI is an upbeat pop song that uses the metaphor of a race to inspire listeners to persevere and pursue their dreams. Its energetic beat and motivational lyrics encourage hard work and resilience, emphasizing the importance of not giving up despite challenges.

Dream Destinations

Japan

I've always been drawn to Japan's rich culture, and my fascination with wearing samurai armor and wielding a katana has fueled my desire to visit. The intricate details of the armor and the artistry behind the katana reflect the profound historical significance embedded in Japanese tradition. Beyond the tangible aspects, Japan's cultural depth and unique blend of modernity and tradition make it an enticing destination for me.

France

Dreaming of a trip to France to witness the Eiffel Tower's grandeur up close and, hopefully, meet my football idol Kylian Mbappe, who plays for the France national team. The allure of both iconic landmarks and the chance to encounter a personal hero makes this journey an exciting prospect.

Spain

Spain's allure, embodied in Madrid's Royal Palace and Prado Museum, and Barcelona's Gaudi masterpieces, captivates me. The blend of history and modern charm fuels my eagerness to explore these iconic landmarks.

Philippines

Eager to explore my own country, the Philippines, with its diverse landscapes and cultural richness. From the Banaue Rice Terraces to the pristine beaches of Palawan, I aim to uncover hidden gems and stories within my homeland.
